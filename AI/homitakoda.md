# Camp Ho Mita Koda Series

## Core Concept

A reusable circular “camp badge” composition with evolving yearly foreground activities.

The background remains relatively stable while the yearly activity changes.

This creates:
- continuity
- collection identity
- reusable visual language
- and efficient constructibility.

---

# Reusable Background Elements

- Circular composition
- Pine framing trees
- Mountains
- Sunrise/sunset
- Winding foreground path
- “Welcome my friend” text

The background intentionally balances:
- visual richness
- and practical buildability.

# Construction Philosophy

- Simplified tree geometry for glue surfaces and durability
- Avoid excessive fragile segmentation
- Foreground activity should remain readable from distance
- Wood grain is allowed to provide visual complexity
- Reusable motifs are encouraged

---

# Important Techniques

- Optional Inkscape detail layers
- Manual tracing over AI reference images
- Veneer inlay concepts for thin lines (ex: bowstring)
- Z-order layering through piece thickness

---

# Lessons Learned

- AI-generated detail often exceeds practical constructibility
- Simplification usually improves finished readability
- The winding path effectively breaks up foreground whitespace
- Reusable backgrounds create stronger collection identity
