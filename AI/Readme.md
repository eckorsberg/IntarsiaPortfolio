# Intarsia Studio Notes

This folder contains the evolving design philosophy, artistic taxonomy, construction techniques, and thematic series notes associated with my intarsia woodworking projects.

The goal is twofold:

1. Preserve continuity between projects and future design discussions.
2. Capture the deeper artistic and construction philosophies that emerge during the creative process.

The work is increasingly organized into thematic “universes” or recurring series rather than isolated projects.

---

# Core Artistic Philosophy

- Constructibility over literal realism
- Simplification is a strength, not a compromise
- Wood grain provides natural visual texture
- Emotional readability matters more than microscopic detail
- AI generation is used for ideation and composition inspiration
- Final segmentation and interpretation are manually redesigned for real woodworking

---

# Long-Term Goal

Develop a coherent body of intarsia work with:
- recurring emotional themes
- reusable visual languages
- durable construction philosophy
- and evolving artistic identity.
