# Cozy Creatures Series

## Core Theme

Whimsical animal scenes emphasizing:
- warmth
- contemplation
- comfort
- quiet companionship
- peaceful solitude

The subjects are less important than the emotional atmosphere.

# Shared Visual Characteristics

- Soft contouring
- Rounded silhouettes
- Relaxed posture
- Emotional calm
- Moderate detail density
- Character-centric composition

---

# Construction Philosophy

- Avoid unnecessary segmentation
- Let contouring create detail
- Use wood grain as texture
- Preserve emotional readability
- Buildability remains important
