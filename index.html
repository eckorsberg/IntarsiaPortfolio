Welcome to my collection of woodworking art.

This is a simple webpage showcasing my woodworking projects of Intarsia, cutting boards and other miscellaneous wood crafts.

Here is a nice article written by the Solon Times in regard to my recent exploration into the art of Intarsia woodworking.

I initially started turning spindles and bowls on a wood lathe but after a while it become seemingly repeatative.
Next came doing scrollsaw work and that was more varied and fulfulling.
But then I took a class at the Marc Adams School of woodworking on something called Intarsia and this art form really appealed to me.
It seems few people practice this form of woodworking but it seems to fit me well.

Here is a nice description of Intarsia

Intarsia is a form of woodworking that involves creating pictures or designs by using different types of woods, each with its own natural color and grain pattern.
The individual pieces of woods are carefully cut and shaped to fit together like a puzzle.
Think of it as painting with wood.
