Guardian Dragon

Pattern Designer: Loading...

Primary Wood Types Used: Pink Ivory, Yellowheart, Spalted Hackberry, Redheart, Cedar, Maple, Aspen, Ebony, Cherry

Description: This is a atunning one. Note that I tinted the Spalted Hackberry green.
